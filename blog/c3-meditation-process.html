Pre Meditation Thoughts

Any New Musings?

Meditation has so many forms, physically, mentally, spiritually and emotionally. What seems core to all these states is the concept of breathing.

Personally, I don't breathe "enough"; and I need to be more aware of my breathing in response to different contexts - especially those influenced by physical exertion and emotionally charged.

This can have a massive impact on other areas in our lives if not managed. In the words of my mentor - "acknowledge with honesty, accept with integrity, then let it go..."
